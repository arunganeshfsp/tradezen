Reading the Profile
POC — Price with most time/TPOs. Magnetic level — market returns here.
Value Area — 70% of trading activity. Price inside VA = accepted range.
IB (A+B) — First 60-min range. Wide IB = trending day. Narrow = range day.
Single Prints — Only one period touched. Shows fast directional move, often fills later.
Poor High/Low — Multiple TPOs at extreme. Unfinished auction — likely revisit.
Naked POC — Prior day's POC not yet revisited. Acts as magnet until filled.
