King Fisher Strategy

Camarilla S1 · EMA Crossover · RSI Confirmation

Waits for price to pierce the Camarilla S1 level, then confirms a reversal with EMA 9/21 crossover and RSI momentum. Fires a CE or PE entry signal with exact strike, premium, stop-loss and target — all in real time during the 9:15–13:00 window.

5-Pillar Framework · Nifty 100 · Weekly Scans

A disciplined 5-pillar framework designed for Nifty 100 swing trades. Run batch scans every Friday, track open positions with live P&L, and receive HOLD or EXIT signals as market conditions evolve over days to weeks.

Osprey Strategy

CE-PE Premium Δ · VWAP Alignment · Risk Reference

Tracks CE and PE premium change from the 9:15 AM open. When one side expands past +10% while the other contracts, the crossover signals a directional setup — with reference levels, stop reference, and risk estimate for one lot.
